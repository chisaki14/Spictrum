How to use?
  1. Add your favorite music to your playlist (you may add multiple items at once)
  2. The items will be added to your playlist
  3. Enjoy your favorite music :)
Additional command
  • Horizontal arrow key to increase or decrease current track played duration
  • Vertical arrow key to increase or decrease audio volume
  • Press Space to pause or resume the audio
  • Press 'Q' to listen to the previous track
  • Press 'E' to listen to the next track
